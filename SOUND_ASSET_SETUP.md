# Sound Asset Setup

v0.5.2 disables all placeholder sound playback by default. The previous IDs caused Studio errors because they were not valid Sound assets, and the looping ambience was annoying.

When you want sound back, use Roblox Creator Store audio assets only. Search for short, clean, non-music sound effects.

## Recommended sound searches

Use Creator Store > Audio and search terms like:

- `short dungeon ambience loop`
- `dark cave ambience loop`
- `spooky room tone loop`
- `soft wind cave loop`
- `monster growl short`
- `creature jump scare sting`
- `trap snap short`
- `metal hit short`
- `sword slash short`
- `coin pickup short`
- `treasure chest open short`
- `magic discovery chime`
- `door stone open short`
- `victory sting short`

## What to avoid

Avoid:
- music tracks
- long songs
- loud looping ringing tones
- meme sounds
- copyrighted/recognizable audio
- assets that are not actually audio Sound assets

## Suggested sound slots

Good first slots to fill later:

- RoomSpookyLoop
- RoomDangerLoop
- EnemyReveal
- TrapSnap
- CombatHit
- TreasureFound
- DiscoveryChime
- DoorOpen
- RouteRollTick
- VictorySting

Keep loops quiet and short. One-shot event sounds should be under about 2 seconds.
